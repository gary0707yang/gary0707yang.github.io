数字营销领域：技能和考证

  2. Posts
  3. 数字营销领域：技能和考证

美国营销协会的一篇文章，列出了以下几个数字营销的网络课程，基本概括了数字营销的全貌：

  • Metrics & Conversions 指标与转化
  • Social & Community 社交与社群
  • Email Marketing 邮件营销
  • User Interface & Experience 用户交互与体验
  • Online Advertising 在线广告
  • SEO 搜索引擎优化
  • Content Marketing 内容营销

详细内容：

指标与转化：

课程包括Common KPIs, Google Analytics, Attribution Analysis, ROI Analysis, A/B Testing, Facebook Insights, Twitter Analytics, and Payment Metrics. 大多是从评测和衡量角度去审查数字营销的效果。像常见的考核标准，分析工具，归因分析，投资回报率分析，A/B测试，Facebook Insights社交分析，Twitter分析，付费指标。

课程列表：

  1. Online Marketing Foundations (3h 45min)
  2. Learning Conversion Rate Optimization (1h 30min)
  3. Google Analytics Essential Training (3h 45min)
  4. Advanced Google Analytics (3h 46min)

社交与社群：

学习如何提高社交曝光度，创作运营策略和有吸引力的内容，理解品牌意识，衡量社交利润点。话题包括Content Marketing, Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram, Pinterest & Snapchat, YouTube, Social Content, Influencer Strategies, Brand Reputation, Blogging, Crisis & Community, Social Employees, and Viral Marketing.

课程列表：

  1. Social Media Marketing: Managing Online Communities (51min)
  2. Social Media Marketing: Facebook and Twitter (1h 36 min)
  3. LinkedIn For Business (1h 33min)

邮件营销：

最大化邮件营销的效果，理解优化拓宽目标群体，分析客户数据和互动。话题包括：Campaign Development, List Management, Performance Metrics, Personalization Tools, HTML Newsletters, Marketing Automation, and CRM.

课程列表：

Optimizing Marketing Emails (1h 06min)

用户交互与体验：

学习通过提升用户体验来提高利润和投资回报率，通过多中数字渠道吸引用户，建设理想的网站和移动端体验，为营销和推广创作富媒体内容。主题包括 Overall Digital Strategy, CRO, Web Design, eCommerce, Inbound Marketing, HTML & CSS, Photoshop, Online Video, Mobile Platforms, Build/Buy an App, and Location-Based.

课程列表：

Web Design Fundamentals (3h 38min)

在线促销推广：

学习通过关键词，目标性推广，社会化推广，以及赞助内容吸引受众兴趣。主题包括： Overall Digital Strategy, CRO, Web Design, eCommerce, Inbound Marketing, HTML & CSS, Photoshop, Online Video, Mobile Platforms, Build/Buy an App, and Location-Based.

课程列表：

  1. Mobile Marketing Foundations (3h 35min)
  2. Google AdWords Essential Training (2h 56min)
  3. Facebook Marketing: Advertising (1h 22min)
  4. Marketing and Monetizing on YouTube (3h 10min)
  5. AdWords and Analytics: Remarketing (57min)

搜索引擎优化：

学习提高搜索引擎曝光度，通过优化关键词和元数据提高搜索引擎排名，通过SEO来提高客户回馈度和品牌体验。主题包括：SEO Tools & Metrics, SEO Link Building, SEO For Localization, Keyword Strategy, Video SEO, Technical SEO, SEO for eCommerce, SEO & Content Marketing, and Reputation Management.

课程列表：

  1. SEO Foundations (3h 27min)
  2. SEO Keyword Strategy (4h 14min)

内容营销：

深入了解如何创作促销和衡量内容的价值和相关度，吸引目标受众，提高参与度。话题包括：Lead generation, Content Marketing, Enterprise Marketing, Small Business Marketing, Branding, Newwsletters, blogs

课程列表：

  1. Content Marketing Foundations (2h 1min)
  2. Content Marketing: Blogs (1h 05min)
  3. Content Marketing: Newsletters (12min 05sec)
  4. Content Marketing: Staying Relevant (18min 12sec)
